Dauphine's Revival is a new band from the Washington D.C. area with a mix of rock dynamics and jazz progression and with an overall Grunge sound. This band has the guitar talent of Eric Clapton and song writing capability of Kurt Cobain. Along side him, a drummer with the rhythm of Dave Grohl. Finally, the bassist has the knowledge of music like Getty Lee with the technique of Ron Carter. Washington Post dubbed Dauphine's Revival as "The saviours of rock for this generation". Dauphine's Revival is the next big thing!
